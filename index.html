"Make in India" is a flagship initiative launched by Prime Minister Narendra Modi with the aim of transforming India into a global manufacturing powerhouse. Launched in September 2014, the initiative seeks to promote indigenous manufacturing, attract foreign investment, and create employment opportunities across various sectors.

As Prime Minister of India, I salute the resilience and unity of our great nation, embodying the spirit of progress and diversity. Together, let us continue to march forward towards a brighter, more prosperous future for all. Jai Hind!
